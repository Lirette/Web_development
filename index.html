Poulet DG means “chicken for the Director General”. It is a famous Cameroonian dish. A lot of people find it very delicious. It is usually served on special occasions in Cameroon.

Like poulet DG, Ndole is also a famous traditionnal Cameroonian food. If you go to a Cameroonian event, you will most likely see this on the menu. Ndole is made primarily of bitter leaf and groundnut.
